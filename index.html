Countertop Materials We Install

NexusxHome works with all major countertop materials, providing expert installation regardless of your material choice. Our installers are trained in the specific requirements and best practices for each material type, ensuring optimal results and longevity.

Granite Countertops

Natural granite offers unmatched durability and unique patterns. Each slab is one-of-a-kind, providing distinctive character to your space. Granite resists heat, scratches, and stains when properly sealed, making it ideal for busy kitchens and high-use areas.

Quartz Countertops

Engineered quartz combines natural stone with advanced polymer technology for exceptional strength and consistency. Non-porous surfaces resist bacteria and staining without requiring sealing. Available in extensive color ranges and patterns including marble-look options.

Marble Countertops

Classic marble brings timeless elegance with distinctive veining patterns. While requiring more maintenance than other materials, marble's beauty and sophistication make it a preferred choice for bathrooms and specialty applications where aesthetics are paramount.

Laminate Countertops

Modern laminate offers affordable versatility with countless design options. Today's laminates feature improved durability and realistic stone and wood patterns. Perfect for budget-conscious projects without sacrificing style or functionality.

Solid Surface Materials

Seamless solid surface countertops provide smooth, non-porous surfaces ideal for both residential and commercial applications. Repairable and renewable, these materials offer long-term value with minimal maintenance requirements.

Butcher Block Countertops

Natural wood countertops add warmth and character to kitchens. We install various wood species with proper sealing and finishing to ensure durability. Ideal for food preparation areas and creating inviting, organic aesthetics.

Our Installation Process

NexusxHome follows a systematic, proven installation process that ensures exceptional results on every project. Our methodical approach minimizes disruption to your daily routine while maximizing quality and efficiency.

Step 1: Initial Consultation

We begin with a comprehensive consultation to understand your vision, requirements, and budget. Our experts assess your space, discuss material options, and provide professional recommendations tailored to your specific needs and preferences.

Step 2: Precise Measurements

Our technicians take detailed measurements of your space using advanced measuring tools and techniques. Accuracy at this stage is critical for ensuring perfect fit and minimizing material waste. We create detailed templates for complex installations.

Step 3: Material Selection & Ordering

Once measurements are confirmed, we assist with final material selection and place orders with our trusted suppliers. We coordinate delivery schedules to align with your project timeline and ensure materials arrive in perfect condition.

Step 4: Site Preparation

Before installation day, we prepare your space by protecting surrounding areas, removing old countertops if necessary, and ensuring the substrate is level and properly supported. Proper preparation is essential for successful installation.

Step 5: Professional Installation

Our certified installers carefully position and secure your new countertops, ensuring perfect alignment and level placement. We handle all cutting, fitting, and sealing with precision. Sink and fixture installations are completed with meticulous attention to detail.

Maintenance & Care Guidelines

Proper maintenance ensures your countertops remain beautiful and functional for decades. NexusxHome provides comprehensive care instructions specific to your material choice, helping you protect your investment and maintain optimal appearance.

Daily Cleaning Practices

Most countertop materials require only simple daily cleaning with mild soap and water. Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive cleaners that can damage surfaces or remove protective sealants. We provide specific cleaning product recommendations for your material type.

Stain Prevention

Promptly wipe up spills, especially acidic substances like wine, coffee, or citrus juices. While most modern countertops resist staining, quick cleanup prevents potential issues. Use cutting boards and trivets to protect surfaces from scratches and heat damage.

Sealing Requirements

Natural stone countertops typically require periodic sealing to maintain stain resistance. We provide initial sealing during installation and offer guidance on resealing schedules. Our team can perform professional resealing services to ensure continued protection.

Long-Term Care

With proper care, quality countertops can last a lifetime. We offer maintenance services including professional cleaning, resealing, and minor repairs. Our care guides help you identify potential issues early and maintain your countertops' beauty year after year.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take?

Most residential countertop installations are completed in one to two days, depending on the size and complexity of the project. We provide accurate timeline estimates during consultation and work efficiently to minimize disruption to your home.

Do you handle sink installation?

Yes, we provide complete sink installation services including undermount, drop-in, and farmhouse sink styles. Our installers ensure proper sealing and alignment for leak-free performance. We can also reconnect plumbing fixtures or coordinate with your plumber.

What about existing countertop removal?

We handle removal and disposal of existing countertops as part of our service. Our team carefully removes old materials to prevent damage to cabinets and surrounding areas. Disposal is included in our project pricing with no additional fees.

Can you match existing countertops?

We can often match or closely approximate existing countertops for additions or repairs. Natural stone matching can be challenging due to unique variations, but we work diligently to find the closest possible match from available materials.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we work with financing partners to offer flexible payment options for qualified customers. Our team can discuss available financing plans during consultation to help make your countertop project affordable and manageable within your budget.

What warranty do you provide?

We offer comprehensive warranties covering both materials and installation workmanship. Warranty terms vary by material type, but all installations include minimum one-year workmanship guarantees. Material warranties are provided by manufacturers and can extend up to lifetime coverage.
